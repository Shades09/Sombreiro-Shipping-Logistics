Who We Are

A Dedicated Nigerian Maritime Services Company

Sombreiro Shipping & Logistics Ltd is a Nigerian-owned and operated maritime services company providing specialist ship agency, husbandry, protective agency, ship spares logistics, ship management, and cargo documentation services to vessel owners, operators, charterers, and cargo interests calling at Nigerian ports.

We are built on the premise that effective maritime agency in Nigeria requires more than an international licence — it demands deep local relationships, regulatory familiarity, and boots-on-the-ground responsiveness that only a truly local partner can provide. Our team brings that expertise to every port call, every vessel, and every client relationship.

Our operations are anchored in the Niger Delta region — the heart of Nigeria's oil and gas maritime corridor — with service coverage extending to all major commercial ports nationwide.
