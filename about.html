Our Story

Nigerian roots,
maritime heritage

From the waterways of the Niger Delta to international maritime standards our story is one of local pride and professional ambition.

Sombreiro Shipping & Logistics Ltd was founded with a clear mandate to bring world-class maritime agency services to Nigerian ports, backed by the kind of local knowledge and relationships that only a truly Nigerian company can build.

Our name reflects our roots. The Sombreiro River is one of the Niger Delta's great waterways a symbol of the commerce, movement, and resilience that has defined this region for generations. That heritage informs everything we do.

Our operations are built on the principle that international maritime standards and deep local expertise are not mutually exclusive they are complementary. We apply the same rigor and professionalism expected of any leading global agency, while offering something no multinational can replicate: a team that genuinely knows Nigeria's ports, people, and regulatory environment.

Maritime agency is, at its core, a relationship business. Our clients trust us to represent their interests at Nigerian ports with port authorities, customs, terminal operators, and regulators. That trust is built through consistency, transparency, and a genuine commitment to the success of every vessel we handle.

We value long-term relationships over transactional wins. Every principal we work with is treated as a partner, not a ticket number.

We are Sombreiro Shipping & Logistics Ltd a 100% Nigerian-owned maritime services company operating across Nigeria's major ports and oil & gas terminals. Our team delivers six core service lines ship agency, husbandry, protective agency, ship spares logistics, ship management, and cargo documentation with 24/7 availability and a commitment to excellence on every port call.

Our Core Strengths

The Sombreiro DNA

Our company is built on three pillars of maritime expertise — Ship Agency, Husbandry & Logistics, and Protective Services. Together, they form a fully integrated offer for vessel owners and operators calling at Nigerian ports.

Ship Agency

We are a specialist ship agent for Nigerian ports managing all pre-arrival, in-port, and post-departure formalities on behalf of ship owners, operators, and charterers. Our NPA and NIMASA relationships ensure smooth, compliant port calls every time.

Husbandry & Logistics

From crew changes and provisions to ship spares clearance and cargo documentation we provide the full spectrum of vessel support services that keep ships moving efficiently through Nigerian waters.

Protective Services

Our protective agency capability serves ship owners, P&I clubs, and underwriters who need independent, on-the-ground representation at Nigerian ports monitoring cargo operations, noting protests, and reporting directly to principals.
