Our Story

Born from the Sombreiro River, Built for the World

The Sombreiro River has been a lifeline of the Niger Delta for centuries — a waterway that connects communities, drives commerce, and embodies the resilience of our people. That heritage is the foundation of everything we do.

Sombreiro Shipping & Logistics Ltd was founded to bring that local depth of knowledge to international maritime standards — providing ship owners, operators, and cargo interests with an agency partner who truly understands Nigeria's ports, people, and waterways.
